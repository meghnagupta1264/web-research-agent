# Research Report: India startup ecosystem funding trends 2025

## Summary
The Indian startup ecosystem witnessed significant funding in 2025, with a total of $10.5 billion raised, solidifying its position as the world's third-largest startup market. The ecosystem is experiencing rapid evolution, driven by increasing investor participation and government-backed initiatives.

## Key Findings
* Indian startups raised $10.5 billion in funding in 2025
* The startup ecosystem in India is the world's third-largest
* Top sectors driving growth include AI, deep tech, and fintech
* Funding trends show growth but continued concentration
* The ecosystem is expected to continue growing with a focus on execution-led growth and selective funding
* Key trends shaping the ecosystem include AI, quantum, chip breakthroughs, and Big Tech investments

## Conclusion
The Indian startup ecosystem demonstrated resilience and growth in 2025, with significant funding and a strong position in the global startup market. As the ecosystem continues to evolve, it is likely to be shaped by emerging technologies like AI, quantum, and chip breakthroughs, as well as investments from Big Tech companies. With a focus on execution-led growth and selective funding, the Indian startup ecosystem is poised for continued growth and innovation in the coming years.
